- **Pain Point:** Teachers spend 2+ hours manually entering 40 grades → errors, delays, stress.  
- **Pain Point:** No standard format → confusion, missed entries.  
- **Value Delivered:**  
  - Upload Excel → **40 students processed in 1.8 seconds**  
  - One-click template download → no confusion
